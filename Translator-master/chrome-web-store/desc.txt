Translate Hebrew to English and English to Hebrew using morfix engine.

Select text on any webpage, then click the Morfix button to view the definition of your selection. 
You can choose from extension settings to see the results in a popup balloon over the web page when you select the text.
Moreover, you can right click on your selection and a menu item will appear directs you to morfix page for that term.
In case the word is not found - Morfix will suggest you correction spelling.
